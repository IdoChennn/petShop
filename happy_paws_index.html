About us

Happy Paw is the largest pet retailer here in London and we sell all kinds of health pets, such as cat, dog, bird and etc. At Happy Paw, we love pets, and we believe pets make us better people. Every day with every connection, Happy Paw's passionate associates help bring pet parents closer to their pets so they, together, can live more fulfilled lives. This vision impacts everything we do for our customers, the way we support our associates and how we give back to our communities.
